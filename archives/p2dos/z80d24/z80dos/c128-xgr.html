C128-XGR.LBR contains a new Z3 Termcap for the Commodore 128 with C128 line graphics. Programs like ZDT12+ will print line graphic boxes. Underline, blink and reverse attributes are also available. The 50 ms CL delay was removed, since the 128 doesn't seem to need it. Randy Winchester, 9/22/92.
